How the sprint works

Our "Rapid Task Elimination™" approach uses a predefined catalog of proven, high-impact automations selected for your business — deployed in 7 days with zero fluff and zero scope creep.

You fill out a short intake form. We map your best-fit automations from our proven quick-win library and deliver a written proposal outlining exactly what we'll automate and why — before we build anything.

Days 3–7

Build & Deploy
