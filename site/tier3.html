The Process

How the 21 days unfold

Our "Total Operation Automation™" combines deep operational discovery, fully custom AI development, core system integration, and an embedded ongoing partnership.

Days 1–5

In-Person Discovery

We come to your offices. Live sessions with your team to map every key workflow, quantify time value, and define transformation priorities. Not a Zoom call — real presence, real accountability.

Days 6–18

Build, Integrate & Test

Full custom automation development, core system integration, and internal testing with your team. We build against the workflows we discovered in person — fully tailored, not adapted from a template.

Days 19–21

In-Person Launch & Onboarding

We return to your offices for live tool walkthroughs, team onboarding, and go-live. Every key user leaves day 21 knowing exactly what the tools do and how to use them.

Ongoing

Embedded AI Partnership

Human support during work hours, continuous chatbot support, ongoing adaptation and minor redevelopment as your operation scales. Your AI capability grows with your business rather than degrading over time.
