The Process

How the 9 weeks unfold

Our "Value-Share Workflow Automation™" model ties our fee directly to measurable time savings. We only win when you win.

Weeks 1–2

Discovery & Scoping

Live synchronous sessions to map your workflows, identify automation targets, and define the time value baseline. This is where we establish what your team's time is worth — the number our fee is calculated from.

Weeks 3–8

Build & Integrate

Custom automations built and integrated into your core systems. Your team is onboarded with live walkthroughs as tools are deployed, so there's no big-bang handover at the end.

Week 9

Launch & Handoff

Go-live, final training, and your Efficiency Tracker Dashboard is activated. From this point, the dashboard tracks hours saved and time value recovered — the exact data your monthly fee is calculated from.

Ongoing

Continuous Optimization

AI chatbot support and continuous adaptation as your workflows evolve. No redevelopment fees, no stale tools gathering dust. As long as you're a client, your automations stay current with your operation.
